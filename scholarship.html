Scholarships & Financial Aid

Explore available scholarships and funding opportunities for engineering students

About Scholarships

The UDSF M-DIT is committed to supporting deserving students through various scholarship programs. We offer financial assistance to help students focus on their studies and achieve academic excellence. Our scholarship programs are designed to recognize merit, support financial need, and encourage excellence in engineering education.

Available Scholarship Programs

Merit Scholarship

For students with outstanding academic performance (CGPA 8.5+). Renewable based on academic performance.

Eligibility: CGPA 8.5+ | Annual

Apply Now

Need-Based Scholarship

Financial assistance for students from economically disadvantaged backgrounds. Requires income certificate.

Eligibility: Income-based | Annual

Apply Now

Excellence Scholarship

For students excelling in both academics and extracurricular activities. Requires portfolio submission.

Eligibility: CGPA 8.0+ + Activities | Annual

Apply Now

Research Scholarship

For students engaged in research projects and publications. Supports academic research activities.

Eligibility: Research Projects | Annual

Apply Now

Application Process

1

Check Eligibility

Review the eligibility criteria for the scholarship program you wish to apply for. Ensure you meet all requirements before proceeding.

2

Gather Documents

Collect all required documents including academic transcripts, income certificates (if applicable), recommendation letters, and identification proof.

3

Submit Application

Fill out the application form completely and submit it along with all required documents to the UDSF office or through the online portal.

4

Review & Selection

Applications will be reviewed by the scholarship committee. Shortlisted candidates may be called for an interview or additional documentation.
